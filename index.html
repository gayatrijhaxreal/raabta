The Meaning of Raabta

One word. Countless verses.

"Raabta" is a word from Urdu and Hindi meaning — connection, contact, bond. In poetry, that connection becomes voice, memory, and emotion. It is not merely linguistic; it carries the human heart across languages and cultures.

When a poet speaks what many feel but few can say, when a ghazal lingers in the mind, when a room listens in silence — that is Raabta.

रा
Raabta (noun)
Connection · Contact · Bond · Verse

A word prevalent in Hindi, Urdu and Persian that captures the depth of human relationships. For us, it also carries the music of poetry — this is our name, and this is our purpose.

About Us

Raabta Poetry Foundation

Raabta Poetry Foundation is a dynamic literary and cultural organization dedicated to promoting poetry, shayari, and creative expression. The word "Raabta" means connection, and that is at the heart of everything we do: connecting people through words, emotions, and art.

We provide a vibrant platform for emerging and established poets, writers, and performers to showcase their talent, share their voices, and engage with a wider audience. Our goal is to make poetry and literature more accessible, relevant, and impactful in today's fast-paced digital world.

Our Vision

To become a leading global poetry platform that nurtures creativity, celebrates diverse voices, and builds meaningful human connections through literature and art.

Our Mission
  • To support and promote emerging poets and writers.
  • To organize high-quality literary events, open mic sessions, and cultural programs.
  • To create opportunities for artistic collaboration and growth.
  • To bring poetry and creative expression closer to modern audiences.

What We Do

Raabta Poetry Foundation is more than just a poetry organization. It is a growing community of passionate individuals who believe in the power of words to inspire, heal, and transform.
